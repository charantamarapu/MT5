A beginner-friendly guide to how the price gap between Gold Spot (XAUUSD) and Gold Futures (GOLD.JUN26) creates reliable, daily intraday profit opportunities.

Concept 1

What is Spot Price?

The price you pay to buy Gold right now, at this exact moment.

🏦

XAUUSD

XAU = Gold, USD = Dollar. This is the live market price of 1 troy ounce of Gold in US Dollars right now. If XAUUSD = $3,200, you can buy 1 oz of Gold for $3,200 this instant.

⚡

Instant Delivery

Spot = "on the spot". The deal is settled immediately. No future dates, no contracts. Just current live price.

📊

Moves 24/5

XAUUSD trades continuously from Monday morning (Sydney open) to Friday night (New York close). Price changes every millisecond.

Concept 2

What is a Futures Contract?

An agreement to buy or sell Gold at a fixed price on a future date.

📋

GOLD.JUN26 Example

This is a Gold Futures contract that expires in June 2026. When you trade it now, you are agreeing to a Gold price that will be settled on that date.

📅

Has an Expiry Date

Every futures contract has an expiry date. On that day, the contract must settle — meaning the price must equal the actual real-world Gold price (Spot price).

💰

Why Is It Higher?

Futures price includes the cost of carrying Gold (storage, insurance, financing) until the expiry date. So Futures is usually slightly higher than Spot.

Think of it like a pre-order — you lock in a price today for delivery later. The pre-order price is slightly higher because of the wait.

Concept 3

Spot vs Futures — The Gap

Both track the same Gold price, but they are never exactly equal — there is always a small gap between them.

🔑 The Key Law

This gap is never infinite. It is controlled by market forces. Because the Futures contract expires in June 2026, on that exact day the Futures price MUST equal the Spot price — otherwise traders would do unlimited arbitrage. This is a mathematical certainty.

Concept 4

The Convergence Guarantee

As the expiry date gets closer, the gap between Spot and Futures shrinks to zero.

📅 Today ← 2 Months → 📅 June 2026 Expiry

The gap starts at ~$30–50 and slowly shrinks. On expiry day: Gap = $0. This is not a prediction — it is a rule of markets.

Concept 5

How the Gap Moves Every Day

The gap is not static. It fluctuates during active trading sessions and calms down during quiet hours.

🌙 Quiet Hours

Gap stays flat around $30. Low movement. Not ideal for trading.

🇬🇧 London Session

Gap starts widening. More volatility. Good trade setups appear.

🇺🇸 NY Session / Overlap

Maximum fluctuation! Gap can swing $0 to $50. Best trade opportunities.

Why This Works

No Swap. No Overnight Risk. Clean Profits.

🚫

No Swap Fees

Swap is a fee brokers charge for holding a trade overnight. Since our trades are opened and closed the same day, we pay zero swap. All profit stays with you.

🛡️

Market Neutral

We buy one and sell the other simultaneously. If Gold price crashes or spikes, both legs move together. We are only exposed to the gap movement, not Gold direction.

🧲

Guaranteed Boundary

The 2-month expiry acts as a mathematical magnet. The gap can never run away to $500 because arbitrageurs would crush it instantly. This keeps our risk bounded.
